Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my package? ▼
Yes! You can upgrade anytime. For downgrades, we'll adjust your next billing cycle. No penalties.
What's included in "Ad Budget Management"? ▼
We manage your ad campaigns, optimize spending, handle A/B testing, and provide daily monitoring. The ad budget (₹8k-10k) is separate and goes directly to Meta/Google.
Do you offer refunds? ▼
We offer a 7-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your first month (minus ad spend already used).
How quickly will I see results? ▼
Ads: 2-4 weeks. SEO: 2-3 months for significant results. Social Media: Engagement immediately, growth after 4-6 weeks.
Can I customize a package? ▼
Absolutely! Starter and Professional packages are flexible. For custom combinations, book a consultation and we'll create the perfect package for you.
What's your onboarding process? ▼
1) Free consultation call (30 min), 2) Proposal & agreement (24 hrs), 3) Onboarding call (48 hrs), 4) Work begins. Total: 3-5 days.
What if I need to pause or stop? ▼
Starter: Cancel anytime. Professional/Premium: 30-day notice required. We'll ensure smooth handover of all your accounts and assets.
